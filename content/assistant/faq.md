## What is this portfolio trying to show?

The site is meant to show how Francisc frames ambiguous work, translates it into structured artifacts, and delivers systems that hold up after handover.

## What kinds of roles does the portfolio support?

The material is strongest for technical program management, product or platform roles with systems responsibility, workflow-heavy AI adoption work, documentation-heavy platform work, and security-aware web platform work.

## What should a reader look at first?

If the reader wants delivery evidence, start with the work case studies. If they want thought process and trade-off framing, start with the writing. If they want role-fit evidence, use the resume page and role-specific resumes.

## What should the assistant avoid claiming?

The assistant should avoid claims that are not directly supported by public material, including invented metrics, speculative role claims, or private project details.
